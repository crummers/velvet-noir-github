About Us

Hello and a warm welcome from Velvet Noir. We pride ourselves on keeping your dance floor full all night, whether it's the happiest day of your life, your corporate event or maybe even your birthday celebration.

Our members have almost two decades of combined knowledge and experience performing at weddings and corporate events all across Scotland. You can rely on us to keep your guests entertained and make your day one to remember.

Our #1 goal when working with our clients is to provide an unforgettable, personalized experience that emphasizes our clients' vision. We offer first-dance requests for brides & grooms and friendly assistance with planning your event entertainment. Check our availability here

Members

Meet the band!

Chelsea Larkins

Chelsea's incredible vocal ability has been astounding guests at weddings and events all across Scotland. A truly exceptional vocalist, you can be sure that Chelsea can cover that special song you've been dreaming of having as your first dance.

Stephen Murphy

Some people sing, some people play guitar. This man does both with talent and style! Stephen and Chelsea compliment each other vocally to cover a wide range of songs, keeping everyone dancing from the start of your night till the buses arrive!

Marc Carruthers

Holding down that low-end, Marc provides one half of the Velvet Noir rhythm section. He likes nothing more than tight grooves and deep tones. He's sure to keep you on your feet while you're getting down on the dancefloor!

Richard Smeaton

Velvet Noir's resident tub thumper sure knows how to keep a beat. Richard is the other half of our rhythm section and always pulls everything together to make your evening entertainment truly one to remember!
